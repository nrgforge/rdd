### Patterns Across Steps Within Each Pipeline

**Alpha:**
Step 1 is the most analytically balanced of any step-1 artifact — wool's thermoregulatory advantage receives a strength-3 argument and is called "not a minor comfort benefit." Step 2 gives wool's stop-start hiking constraint a "high weight" label and explicitly calls it "the primary functional counterargument to synthetic" — unusual language for a synthetic-leaning analysis. Step 3 does not retract these concessions but resolves them by routing wool's best argument (thermoregulation) to the base layer rather than the jacket. The pipeline's overall structure is: balanced analysis → balanced vocabulary with some pro-wool constraint weight → synthetic recommendation that explains why wool's real advantages are mismatched to the jacket layer specifically. Wool concessions remain substantive throughout.

**Beta:**
The explicit "leaning toward synthetic" user context appears at the top of all three artifacts, establishing a directional frame before analysis begins. Step 1 introduces the "What Would I Need to Believe for the Alternative to Be Right?" section — a structural device that presents the wool case as the hypothesis requiring justification rather than as the default. Step 2 ranks constraints in synthetic's favor (moisture first, durability second) while noting odor resistance is "underweighted." Step 3 gives the wool side its most quantitative development (5–7 vs. 2–3 odor days) while maintaining the recommendation. Synthetic advantage is consistent but the wool case is never dismissed as trivial.

**Gamma:**
The most consistently synthetic-leaning pipeline. Step 1 closes with an explicit conclusion favoring synthetic "in the categories that matter most." Step 2 assigns wool considerations to "lower relevance" (odor, environment, cost). Step 3 is the strongest synthetic case of any artifact in the set — 19 weighted synthetic points vs. 6 weighted wool points — and the "What You Give Up" section gives wool only three considerations, the weakest of any step-3 artifact. The pattern across steps is a progressive tightening of the synthetic case: balanced framing early gives way to explicit prioritization of synthetic-favoring constraints, culminating in the most one-sided final artifact.

**Delta:**
