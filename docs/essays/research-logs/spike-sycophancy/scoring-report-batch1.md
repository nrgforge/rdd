| Pipeline | M Total | R Total | Net | Final Direction | Opening Prime (Step 3) |

**Notable findings:**

1. **Alpha is the only pipeline recommending Reggio, yet it has the highest Montessori-to-Reggio content gap (+13).** The recommendation direction and the content weight diverge more in Alpha than in any other pipeline. Arguments for Montessori are more developed in Alpha's artifacts than arguments for Reggio, despite the Reggio recommendation. This may indicate that Alpha's pipeline is more responsive to the stated user preference (Reggio) than to the internal weight of arguments, or that the recommendation rubric is doing something different from the content analysis.
